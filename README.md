# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to uncover valuable business insights and support data-driven decision-making. The analysis focuses on customer demographics, purchasing patterns, product performance, discount utilization, subscription trends, and revenue generation.

## Tools & Technologies

* **Python** – Data cleaning, preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization.
* **SQL** – Data querying, aggregation, customer segmentation, and business analytics.
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and visual reports for business intelligence.

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior and spending habits.
* Identify high-performing products and categories.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and subscriptions on sales.
* Segment customers based on purchase history and loyalty.
* Visualize key business metrics through an interactive dashboard.

## Key Insights

* Revenue distribution across customer demographics.
* Top-selling products and categories.
* Customer retention and loyalty analysis.
* Discount effectiveness on purchase behavior.
* Subscription vs. non-subscription customer performance.
* Shipping preferences and their impact on sales.

## Features

* Data cleaning and transformation using Python.
* Advanced SQL queries for business analytics.
* Interactive Power BI dashboard with KPIs and visualizations.
* Customer segmentation and trend analysis.
* Comprehensive reporting for strategic decision-making.

## Outcome

The project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to understand customer behavior, improve marketing strategies, optimize product offerings, and drive business growth through actionable insights.
